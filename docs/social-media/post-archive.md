## How to Log a Post

After publishing, add a row immediately:
- **Date** — YYYY-MM-DD
- **Platform** — Threads / X
- **URL** — Direct link to the post
- **Product** — Card, set, or sealed product referenced (leave blank if general take)
- **Type** — Commentary / Commitment / Reaction
- **Notes** — Optional: app agreement/disagreement, who you reacted to, outcome if known
