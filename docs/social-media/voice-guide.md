# Future Sight — Content Voice Guide

---

## Core Voice Principles

- **First-person, confident but not arrogant.** You have a point of view. State it.
- **Data-informed, human delivery.** "I've been watching this one for 6 months" beats a bullet list of facts.
- **Always clear on stance.** No wishy-washy "it could go either way." Pick a side.
- **Cite the app when relevant, disagree openly when you diverge.** Showing you'll override the algorithm builds authenticity and trust.
- **Tag original creators on reaction posts.** Drives traffic back, shows respect, earns goodwill.

---

## Three Post Formats

### 1. Investment Commentary

Structure:
```
[Product] — [current market condition in one sentence]

[Future Sight reasoning: scarcity / character IP / historical comps / supply dynamics]

My take: [Buy / Hold / Watch / Avoid] — [one or two sentences of conviction]

[Optional: App says [X], I'm going [Y] anyway — here's why]
```

Example:
```
Prismatic Evolutions ETBs — still inflated from the holiday run but supply is tightening.

This set has the Eevee IP at its peak, pull rates on the Umbreon SAR are brutal, and
there's no reprint confirmed. Historical comps with Evolving Skies suggest the floor
holds even if hype cools.

My take: Buy the dip if you see it under $65. App says Watch — I'm buying anyway.
```

---

### 2. Prediction Commitment

Structure:
```
[Product] — [stance and reasoning]

I'll check back in [timeline]. Logging this one.

[Optional: App score + your agreement/disagreement]
```

After posting: log in `predictions.md` and run `reminder-script.applescript`.

Example:
```
Surging Sparks booster boxes are still available at MSRP at most targets.
Pikachu ex SIR will age well — the character has never lost relevance.

My take: Buy and hold. I'll report back in 1 year.
```

---

### 3. Sphere of Influence Reaction

Structure:
```
[Tag creator handle] — [one-sentence summary of their claim]

[Your research-backed response: agree, push back, or add nuance]

[Your stance on the product, grounded in data]

[Optional CTA: "What do you think?" / "Drop your take below"]
```

Use `reaction-workflow.md` to get OpenClaw to draft this for you.

## Platform Notes

| Platform | Character limit | Best for | Notes |
|----------|----------------|----------|-------|
| Threads | ~500 chars (no hard limit) | Longer takes, conversation | More forgiving, IG crossover audience |
| X/Twitter | 280 chars (free) / longer (paid) | Punchy takes, reactions, reach | Use thread for multi-part posts |

**Post both simultaneously.** Slight wording adjustments for character limits are fine.

---

## What NOT to Do

- Don't hedge. "It might go up but it also might not" is useless.
- Don't post without a clear product reference or stance.
- Don't engage with bad-faith criticism on the post itself — respond to genuine questions only.
- Don't overpost. Quality over volume. One strong take is worth five filler posts.
